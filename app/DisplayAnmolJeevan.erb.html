DETAILS

LIC Anmol Jeevan is a term insurance policy with risk cover of less than 25,00,000 SUM assured.
SUM assured exceeding 25,00,000 can be availed in LIC Amulya Jeevan policy . This Term assured policy provides risk cover at lower of premium. On death within terms SUM assured is payable to nominee, no maturity benefits are applicable.

BENEFITS & FEATURES

Maturity Benefits : NIL

Death Benefits : SUM Assured payable to nominee in case of death within term

Policy Loan : Not Available

Tax Benefits : Tax benefits on premium under section 80C and death claim under section 10 (10D) as per IT Act 1961

Others Benefits : Revival and assignment available


Policy Eligibility Chart
Age entry: 18 to 55 years
Maximum Maturity Age: 65 Years
Min/Max Term: 5 to 25 Years
Minimum and Maximum Sum Assured Rs 5,00,000 to less than Rs. 25,00,000
Premium Payment Yearly, Half-Yearly and Single Premium
